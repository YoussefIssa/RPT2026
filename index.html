Tournament Rules

How it Works

The official rules and format for the Ramadan Padel Tournament 2026.

🤝
General Participation
  • Exclusive Play Access — Participation is strictly limited to Finaira employees
  • Dynamic Duos — Each team must consist of exactly two players
  • Keep it Classy — We're all here for a good time. Please maintain high standards of sportsmanship
  • Be Early — Teams must show up 5 minutes before their scheduled match
🎾
Group Stage — Format
  • Matches consist of four games — "Short Set"
  • If games are tied, a 7-point tie-break set will be played
  • 15-minute match limit — if the timer rings mid-game, finish that game before shaking hands
  • More than 5 minutes late = team is considered withdrawn
🏆
Group Stage — Scoring
  • Win = 2 points
  • Loss = 0 points
  • 8 teams qualify — Top 2 from each group + the 2 best 3rd-place finishers
  • Points tie? The team with the most games won advances
⚡
Knockout Stage
  • Matches consist of six games — "Normal Set"
  • If games are tied, a 7-point tie-break set will be played
  • 25-minute match limit — if time expires during a game, the game must be completed before the match ends
  • Be Early — Teams must show up 5 minutes before their scheduled match
